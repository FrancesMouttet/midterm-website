Directions

Step 7: Cook until bubbles form

Wait until bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake. This usually takes about 2–3 minutes.

Step 8: Flip the pancake

Flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden brown.

Step 9: Serve and enjoy

Serve the pancakes with toppings like syrup, fruit, or butter.
