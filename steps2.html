Directions

Step 4: Combine wet and dry mixtures

Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Mix gently until combined.

Step 5: Heat your pan

Place a pan or griddle on medium heat and lightly grease it.

Step 6: Pour the batter

Scoop the batter onto the pan. Use about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
